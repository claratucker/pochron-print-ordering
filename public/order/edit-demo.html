Self-edit demo

This is the customer-facing editor slot. Filerobot A customer crops and adjusts their photo here; on save we capture the edit as a small recipe and keep it with their original. At approval the studio re-applies that recipe to the full-resolution original — the print never comes from this in-browser preview.

1 · Choose a print size & open the editor

Uses a sample image. In the live flow the source is the customer's uploaded original.

2 · The recipe we store & send to the backend

Edit and hit Save in the editor to see the recipe.

How this attaches to an order

On save, the order item is sent with colorPath: "self" and this recipe as its adjust field. The studio queue then shows a Customer self-edit badge, and approving the photo calls the render endpoint that produces the print-ready file from the original + recipe.
